Checkout service overview

          
checkout-api receives order requests, calls out to the payment provider via PaymentClient, and persists the order once payment is confirmed. It shares a single database connection pool across all endpoints (max 50 connections).

          
Retry policy

          
Payment calls are retried up to 3 times on timeout, with a fixed 5-second wait per attempt. Retries currently hold their database connection open for the entire retry sequence rather than releasing it between attempts.

          
Known limitations

          
The connection pool size was last tuned over a year ago, before checkout volume roughly tripled. There's an open ticket to move to a per-request-scoped connection instead of holding one for the whole retry loop.
